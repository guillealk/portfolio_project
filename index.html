Guillermo Aranda Lopez King

Software Engineer

Description

I am a System Engineer with specialization in Software Architecture, Software Design, and Software Development. I have a bachelor degree in System Engineering from the Universidad Distrital Francisco Jose de Caldas, Colombia. Furthermore, I have recently completed an European master in Software Engineering from two universities: the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid, Spain and the Technische Universität Kaiserslautern, Germany. From my studies, I have gained knowledge in Information Systems, primarily related to Software Engineering, System Modeling Language, UML, Enterprise Architect, Java Development, as well as Embedded System, Model Based System Engineering, Interaction Design, User Experience, Agile Development, Database Management and Programming Languages skills.

For the past three years, I have been working as an intern at different research institutes. First, I worked as GIS Developer Analyst Intern at Fundación Parque Tecnológico de Itaipu-Paraguay where I designed and developed software architecture for different Geographic Information System projects. Then, I worked as Software Architecture Researcher Intern at Fraunhofer IESE, where the focus of my research for my master thesis was on Software Product Line and System Modeling Language for my master thesis. Currently, I am still working as a researcher intern at Fraunhofer IESE in the areas of Internet of Things, Industrial Internet of Things, and Cyber-Physical Systems. The aim of this research is to obtain a better understanding of the mentioned topics and comprehend the possibilities of applying them to the industry.

Therefore, from my working experience, I developed a variety of skills needed for supporting the development and maintenance of software projects, as well as, for working successfully with a diversity of stakeholders, customers, and end-users.
